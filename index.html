Our Story

A refuge in the
heart of the city


Solace Café was born from a simple belief — that everyone deserves a quiet corner to exhale, sip something beautiful, and feel at peace. Nestled in Bangalore's vibrant heartbeat, we are that corner.

From ethically sourced single-origin beans brewed with care, to warm wooden nooks where conversations bloom — every detail at Solace is designed to give you exactly what the name promises.
